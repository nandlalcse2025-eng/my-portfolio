Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

Early Life

Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany in 1879. He showed an early interest in mathematics and science.

Major Contributions

His most famous equation is E = mc2, which describes the relationship between mass and energy.
